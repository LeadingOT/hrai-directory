# What is AI Resume Screening

**AI resume screening is the use of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) to automate the initial review and filtering of job applications, ranking candidates based on their predicted fit for a role.**

The technology works by parsing the information within resumes and application materials (like cover letters or portfolios) and comparing it against a set of criteria defined for the job. This criteria often includes keywords, skills, years of experience, education, certifications, and other relevant patterns. Modern AI systems go beyond simple keyword matching; they use natural language processing (NLP) to understand context, infer skills from project descriptions, and even assess semantic relevance. For example, an AI trained for tech roles can understand that "Python," "Django," and "API development" in a project summary collectively indicate back-end engineering experience, even if the exact phrase "back-end engineer" isn't present.

AI screening is typically deployed at the top of the hiring funnel to manage high-volume applications. A recruiter might upload 500 resumes for a single opening. The AI tool quickly scores and shortlists the top 50 candidates whose profiles most closely align with the job description, saving countless hours of manual review. It can also help reduce unconscious human bias by focusing on objective, job-related data points—though this is highly dependent on how the AI models are trained and configured to avoid perpetuating biases found in historical hiring data.

This technology matters because it directly addresses efficiency and scale in recruitment. For employers, the primary use case is drastically reducing the time-to-review and time-to-hire for high-volume roles, allowing recruiters to focus on engaging with qualified candidates. For job seekers, it means tailoring a resume with relevant keywords and clear achievement statements is critical to passing this initial digital gatekeeper. A practical example is a large retail company using AI to screen thousands of seasonal applicant resumes, instantly filtering for availability, location, and prior cash-handling experience.
